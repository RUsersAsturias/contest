Call for entries for the first R users Asturias data visualisation contest.






About the data:

Data has been obtained from the transparency web of Gijón's city. Original data can be accesed in its data catalog.
Data to be used in the contest is a subset of the fines register from years 2015 to 2017 that has been unified in a single file, which can be downloaded in RData or CSV format.
In this data it can be found the date and hour of the fine, motive, place, fee, discount perceived (if any), speed and limit speed, and, for some cases, exact coordinates of the fine.










About the contest:

1. During February 28th, 2019, the database base will be made publicly available at https://rusersasturias.github.io/contest/ and in the repository. Anybody will be able to participate sending a plot made with R software.

2. Project presentation will take place between March 1st, 2019 and 11:59 PM on April 30th, 2019, sending an e-mail to grupousuariosrasturias@gmail.com with the visualization or the url to it in the prefered format. Name and surname will be specified in the e-mail.

3. This edition's jury is comprised of two assistant professors of the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine and an epidemiologist of the Conselleria de Sanitat Universal i Salut Pública de la Generalitat Valenciana.

4. The jury will issue its opinion on the 31st of May, 2019. The declaration will be made public on the site of the contest, and the winner will be notified by e-mail.

5. The price amount is three hundred euros (300 €).

6. Contest's bases can be accesed in this website.
