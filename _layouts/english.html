First edition: March - April 2019



Data visualization contest with R: first edition


Call for entries for the first R users - Asturias data visualisation contest.



1. Throughout february 28 2019 data base will be made public at https://rusersasturias.github.io/contest/ and in the repository. Anybody will be able to participate sending a plot made with R software.

2. Submissions will be made between March 1 2019 and 23:59 h April 30 2019, sending a mail to grupousuariosrasturias@gmail.com with the visualization or the url to it in the prefered format. Name and surname will be specified in the mail.

3. Jury will be formed by two assistant professors of the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine and an epidemiologist from Conselleria de Sanitat Universal i Salut Pública de la Generalitat Valenciana.

4. The jury will release the veredict on May 31 2019. Resolution will be made public in contest website, and the winner will be contacted by mail.

5. Price will be three hundred euros (300 €).

6. Contest's bases can be accesed in this website.
